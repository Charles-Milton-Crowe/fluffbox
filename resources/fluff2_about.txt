Commands
.
F2 - Toggles the Honour Roll
F3 - Advances the chapter in time by 1 decade.
F4 - Advances the chapter in time by 1 millenium.
F5 - Dropdown toggle menu for showing companies.
F6 - Dropdown toggle menu for showing ranks.
F7 - Presents this page.
F8 - Closes the program.
